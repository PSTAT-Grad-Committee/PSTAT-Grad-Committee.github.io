As an organization, we strive to:
  • provide streamlined communication between graduate students and important departmental information, provide academic support for graduate students
  • engage with the department in social capacities, promote a diverse and equitable environment that encourages respect between all graduate students and faculty

To this end, we have established the following officer positions:
  • Communications and Technology Officer
  • Academic Events Officers
  • Non-Academic Events Officers
  • Diversity and Inclusion Subcommittee
If you would like to join as one (or more) of these positions, feel free to email us or message an officer on our discord server.
Services we provide include:
  • Faculty and Graduate Student Seminars.
  • “Stuff you Missed in Undergrad:” a short lecture series (hosted by grad students) designed to advise students on useful (but perhaps forgotten) topics.
  • Regularly scheduled social events, both among graduate students as well as between graduate students and the faculty
  • Quarterly “Town Hall” meetings, designed to amplify perspectives and concerns of our community and to promote empathy among one another.
  • Graduate Student Office Hours.
  • Faculty Office Hours (note that these are independent of office hours relating to specific classes).
  • Our events will be posted in advance to our calendar, as well as to our discord server.
