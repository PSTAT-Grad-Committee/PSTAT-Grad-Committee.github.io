Treasury

The main goal of the treasury is to oversee Committee expenditure. Duties include drafting and submitting formal budgetary proposals to the department, reimbursement of members (as needed), and any/all other financial duties pertaining to the Committee. Note: Due to COVID-19 and the subsequent online nature of our events, it is expected that the Treasury will be largely non-functional for the foreseeable future.
