Mission Statement

As an organization, we strive to:
  • provide streamlined communication between graduate students and the department,
  • provide academic support for graduate students
  • engage with the department in social capacities,
  • promote a diverse and equitable environment that encourages respect between all graduate students and faculty

Services We Provide

Social Events

Throughout the quarter, we will host a series of social gatherings including Happy Hours, Trivia Nights, Jeapordy, and a series of other fun events. To stay appraised of upcoming events, check out our calendar as well as our discord server.

Town Hall Meetings

We plan on hosting quarterly Town Hall Meetings, designed to amplify perspectives and concerns of our community and to promote empathy among our fellow PSTAT graduate students. All will be Welcome to join!

Stuff you Missed in Undergrad

This series of lectures, hosted weekly, is presented by graduate students and is designed to introduce (and possibly re-introduce) our fellow PSTAT graduate students to some concepts that will prove useful in our graduate careers. Topics will range from mathematics, to probability, to programming, and beyond. All are welcome to attend!
