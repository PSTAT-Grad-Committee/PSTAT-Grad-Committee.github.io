Administrative Panel

The main purpose of the administrative panel is to handle back-end operation of the Committee, and to ensure that the Committee runs smoothly. Duties of the Organizational Secretary include taking meeting minutes and organizing committee meetings, duties of the Communications Officer include overseeing and drafting official emails, and duties of the Technology Officer include providing technological support to students (as well as maintaining the website and discord server).
